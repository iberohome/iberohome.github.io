Why Spain?


Spain is a unique country in that it has a blend of cultures embedded in its history. It is well known for its climate but it has much more to offer than sunshine. The cuisine varies from region to region according to the nature of the land. In the North, the "cocidos" are famous, the centre is known for its "asados" and in the South they fry everything.
